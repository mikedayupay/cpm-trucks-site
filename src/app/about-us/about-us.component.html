About Us

A Great and Long Tradition of Excellence in the Trucking Industry

Central Pacific Motors

Since 1981, Mr. Carlos D. Chua has been setting a tradition of excellence in the trucking industry, both as supplier/importer of all kinds of trucks and other special purpose and commercial vehicles, and as hauler and trucker.

As one of the biggest dealers of cement, he owned and maintained fifty (50) hauler-trucks, which regularly carried and hauled from the Northern Cement Corporation depot to various destination points in Luzon. For this purpose, the Carlo Martin Cement Marketing Corporation was formed and established by Mr. Carlos Chua.

As importer and supplier of all kinds of trucks and other special purpose and commercial vehicles, Central Pacific was formed and established by Mr. Chua. It remains to be one of the most reputable and reliable suppliers of trucks in Metro Manila.
